## Interpretation

- The board-verified GA run reaches `40/40` label accuracy at
  `generation 1` with SSE `4799`.
- The M7.5.3 trained tile reproduces its upstream golden bitmap exactly
  (`0` golden mismatches), but that golden bitmap is
  `37/40` against labels on this test set.
- The evolved INT8 genome is therefore better on this quantized deployment metric:
  it finds a `40/40` solution directly in the hardware-visible
  INT8 search space, while the gradient-trained Q8.8 master quantizes to a
  `37/40` INT8 tile.
- The two 24-byte genomes differ in `8` positions, so the GA is not merely
  preserving the M7.5.3 trained tile.
